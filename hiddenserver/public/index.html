A Hidden Service on Tor

People use Tor hidden services for all kinds of things. Privacy, journalism, censorship circumvention, drugs, murder for hire, you name it! (But mostly it's just an excuse to Bitcoin)

Hidden services use the Onion network and the Tor routing system to:

  1. Hide their location
  2. Anonymize their registration
  3. Provide a global address at to users

Setting up a hidden service is easy. All you need is a local web server, and the free client software provided at the Tor project's website
